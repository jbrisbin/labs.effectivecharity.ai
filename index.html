About the effectivecharity.ai labs

effectivecharity.ai labs is a research initiative dedicated to applying cutting-edge artificial intelligence to the challenge of doing good better.

We believe that thoughtful application of AI can help charitable organizations and individual donors make more informed decisions, ultimately leading to greater positive impact in the world.

Our work spans research, tool development, and collaboration with leading organizations in the effective charity and philanthropy spaces.
